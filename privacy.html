Institutional Privacy Policy

Commitment to Privacy

TERRAQUEST INVESTMENTS - FZCO ("TerraQuest," "we," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and operational confidentiality of our institutional partners, prospective investors, and site visitors. This policy outlines our data processing practices in alignment with UAE Data Protection standards.

Information Collection

We collect specific, voluntarily provided professional data through our digital inquiry forms. This includes:

  • Name and Professional Title
  • Institutional Affiliation
  • Corporate Email Addresses
  • Strategic Areas of Interest and Message Content

Data Utilization

The data we collect is utilized strictly for professional B2B purposes, including:

  • Facilitating direct dialogue and assessing strategic alignment.
  • Distributing requested institutional materials, regulatory disclosures, or platform details.
  • Internal compliance and institutional onboarding protocols.

Data Protection & Third-Party Disclosure

TerraQuest treats all institutional inquiries with strict confidentiality. We do not sell, trade, or independently transfer your personally identifiable information or institutional data to outside parties. This excludes trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our digital infrastructure, provided those parties agree to keep this information strictly confidential and compliant with tier-1 data protection standards.

Inquiries & Data Rights

If you wish to access, correct, or delete any professional information you have submitted to TerraQuest, please initiate a dialogue through our primary contact channels, and your request will be processed in accordance with applicable freezone and international data regulations.
